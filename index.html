In a post apocaliptic world, resources are scarce. The only power-efficient way to build a "modern" air defense system is by using solar powered laser beams! Defend your desert from the enemy air raids for as long as you can! Good luck!

1. To start press anywhere on the game screen.
2. To Lock on target press anywhere on the game screen while the radar ray is pointing the enemy plane.
3. Next, you have to lock the range to the target by clicking anywhere on the screen, while the range indicator is above the enemy!
4. Then, the automated laser system will destroy the incomming threat, while you prepare to lock the next baddie.
5. Beware, the enemy planes are reported to carry tactical nuclear weapons. If they reach you: GAME OVER;
